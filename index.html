FAQ

    How much does it cost?

    Nothing! The event is absolutely free for all participants. We will provide you with the room to hack, sleeping areas, food, Wi-Fi, etc. You just need to be here.

     Who can attend?

    HackWesTX is open to any college student. If you're someone who is willing to learn and wants to create something amazing, no matter what your field is, you definitely should check us out.

     I have never been to a hackathon before, what should I do?

    No worries. We will be providing mentors during the hackathon and running workshops.

     Will Travel Reimbursements be provided?

    We currently do not have enough funding to provide any travel reimbursements. If the situation changes we will notify all registered hackers and update the website.
    How long is the event?

    The event will be 24 hours long and participants can come and go as they please, but are encouraged to stay in the designated building for easy access to resources hackers need. With that said we will have a few extra events like game tournaments and group activities to help keep the blood flowing!

     What should I bring?

    Laptops, chargers, cell phones, and of course passion is helpful. Other useful items include: toiletries, a change of clothes, a pillow, some earplugs and a jacket.

     What if I have a question that is not listed?

    No worries. We will be providing mentors during the hackathon and running workshops

     Code of Conduct

    We are under the MLH Code of Conduct and we expect everyone who participates to follow it. The document describing the code of conduct is here.

    Use this email to ask any other questions: contact@hackwestx.tech
